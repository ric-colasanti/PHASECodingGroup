Web NetLogo

You can link to the NetLogo Web page here or click on title above. The link will take you to a random NetLogo model. You can browse numerous models from Search the Models Library. Or start creating your own by clicking on the "New" button at the top right hand corner.

IMPORTANT

You have to save your model by clicking on the Export buttons. Choose HTML.

To access your saved model just click on the file from your file manager.




Google colab

Google's Colab a product from Google Research. Colab allows anybody to write and execute arbitrary python code through the browser. More technically, Colab is a hosted Jupyter notebook service that requires no setup to use, while providing free access to computing resources.This means that the model can be run by anyone with a Google account and a Chrome or Edge browser. Nothing needs to be installed on their computer.
